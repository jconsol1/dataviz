Consumer Complaint Data in dealing with Navient Solutions

Consumer Complaints Across States from 2020 to 2022
Infogram

By James Consoli

The data above was analyzed by looking at thousands of complaints regarding loans and credit reports filed against Navient Solutions, LLC. This data was pulled from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau and their Consumer Complaint reports. These states had very close numbers in terms of their complaints, and that is no surprise given the states and their respective populations being very similar. Something worth noting from the data is that although the populations of the states are decreasing from left-to-right, the number of consumer complaints increases. This is interesting because in most consumer complaint categories, and in most cases overall, the bigger the state's population, the more consumer complaints they report. Examples include Texas, Florida, California and New York. This data could provide plausible reasoning to look further into the state of Delaware and why they had five complaints in dealing with Navient from 2020-2022.
